Product flow

A high-trust journey from verification to curated date

1. Register
Verify identity

Photo, short video, personal story, and structured questions build a rich profile.

2. Curate
AI + human scan

AI generates a shortlist, then human experts validate fit, seriousness, and quality.

3. Approve
Mutual decision

Each side can review the match and send a greeting or personal letter before meeting.

4. Meet
Date orchestration

The platform confirms the match and proposes the date at a venue chosen by the app.
